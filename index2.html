Mel-Fun Quiz

The Mel-fun Quiz is a quick game that test your coding knowledge in the form of a quiz.
Will you conquer the quiz or will the quiz conquer you?
Let's put your knowledge to the test and let's see.

Quiz Guide

There are 10 questions in total. Each question has 4 possible answers. To pass the quiz, you must get at least 60% and above. If you get less than 60%, you must retake the quiz.
